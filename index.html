About Us

We are a dynamic and forward-thinking team, guided by the principles of truth-seeking, diligence, resilience, and innovation. Under the leadership of Prof. Xinghui Dong , our lab is dedicated to providing robust academic support for research and encouraging our members to engage in pioneering studies. Our research is centered in the field of computer vision, spanning various academic domains, including image segmentation, image enhancement, object detection, and texture perception.

We extend a warm invitation to young and ambitious individuals who are passionate about academic excellence to become a part of our dynamic community. If you are enthusiastic about research and eager to make a meaningful impact, we look forward to welcoming you to our team.

Team

Xinghui Dong

Xinghui Dong, Ph.D., is a Professor and doctoral supervisor at the School of Information Science and Engineering at Ocean University of China. He received his Ph.D. (2014) from Heriot-Watt University in the United Kingdom. Dr. Dong's academic journey includes a significant research period from 2015 to 2021 at the Centre for Imaging Sciences, University of Manchester. His research interests encompass computer vision, visual perception, deep learning, and intelligent testing.

Dr. Dong has authored 46 papers published in top-tier journals and international conferences, including IEEE TPAMI, IEEE TIP, IEEE TVCG, and prestigious conferences like ICCV and ECCV.
